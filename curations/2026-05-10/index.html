The Landing

When things finally touch down

There's a peculiar weight to the moment something finally arrives. After the waiting, the uncertainty, the motion—the landing.

Today's news is full of arrivals. A ship reaches shore after days of viral fear. A ceasefire settles over a war zone. A cold case finds its answer after thirty years of silence. An island nation positions itself between two giants.

The landing changes everything. What happens next is up to us.

Touching ground

Every landing is both an ending and a beginning. The ship docks, but the quarantine starts. The ceasefire holds, but the politics continue. The case closes, but the grief remains. The summit approaches, but the dance is just beginning.

We spend so much time in transit—waiting for the verdict, the arrival, the answer—that we forget the landing is just another threshold.

What matters is what we do once our feet touch ground.
