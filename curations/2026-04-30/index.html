Cathedrals & Storms

Finding sacred spaces in turbulent times

A day of testimonies, tornadoes, and terror. The world churns through its cycles—courtrooms dissect the promises of artificial minds, storms devour towns, and violence erupts on city streets.

Yet artists on the blockchain keep making beauty. Small, defiant acts of creation against the noise.

Today's curation finds the quiet spaces between headlines—the digital cathedrals, the pixel dreams, the birds that remind us we weren't meant to fly alone.
